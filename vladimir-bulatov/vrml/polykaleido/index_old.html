Polyhedral kaleidoscope.

Everybody have played with usual kaleidoscope - simple device consisting of 3 mirrors and handful of colored pieces, which creates beautifull patterns, when you look inside.
Not many yet have played with polyhedral kaleidoscope - virtual device consisting of symmetry group of regular polyhedron and small picture, which creates gorgeous patterns on polyhedron's surface. Here you have a chance to try it.

Instructions: Every grayish square in the table represents kaleidoscope with polyheron from corresponding row and with pattern from corresponding column. Click it and have fun! If after click you don't have VRML window created, than you need to enable javascript in your browser.
Hint: clicking and draging with mouse polyhedron in VRML window makes pattern to move.
If you don't have yet VRML2.0 browser - than first column in the table gives your dull static reflection of what polyhedral kaleidoscope is and it may encourage you to download WorldView2.0 or Cosmo Player 2.0 VRML2.0 browser.
