Sphere eversion

This interactive device visualizes the famous topological process - turning a sphere inside out by means of smooth motions. Slider on the right controls different stages of this process, buttons in front control which part of sphere is visible.

For more details on sphere eversion visit Outside In at Geometry Center. The data for this visualization was produced by modified version of program evert, distributed by Geometry Center.
