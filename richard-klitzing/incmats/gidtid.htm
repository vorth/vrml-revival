As abstract polytope gidtid is isomorphic to sidtid, thereby replacing pentagons by pentagrams. – As such gidtid is a lieutenant.

Gidtid also can be obtained as a blend of sidtid with ditdid, blending out the pentagrams.

This polyhedron is an edge-faceting of the small ditrigonary icosidodecahedron (sidtid).
